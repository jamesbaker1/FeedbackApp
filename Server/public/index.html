Easily crowdsource ideas and infomation from your community.

Get Started: ios Android Slack

Example Use Cases

Retail: Aggregate what your store associates are hearing from customers

It's the sales review meeting and the South Texas district is struggling. Looking at the numbers it's not clear why... so you pull up South Texas in the 'Suggestion Box' dashboard and look at the customer feedback that your district store associates reported over the last week. Bingo! Apparently, a local news story came out about a material in your product and it is scaring customers. You respond to the store associates who reported this issue with the information they need to reassure customers.

Schools: Improve community communications by responding directly to parents who submit or agree with a piece of feedback

There are thousands of parents and community members with ideas for how your schools should be run. Often the ideas are conflicting, it's not clear if the feedback is one person's opinion or something everyone agrees on, a lot of the time it's a misunderstanding that you have an answer to, but don't know which parents need to be told.

The Suggestion Box can help:
The community can vote that they agree or disagree with feedback, so when there are conflicting opinions or it is just one person's opinion that becomes clear to the community.
If there is a misunderstanding, you can respond to the specific community members who submitted (or said they agree) with a piece of feedback.
If similar feedback is submitted again, Suggestion Box can auto-respond for you.

HR: Enable your employees to organically identify, prioritize, and craft great ideas for improving your business

You know your employees have a wealth of knowledge about your business, if you could just tap it...
With Suggestion Box its easy for an employee, in product for example, to submit an idea for improving their product. Sales would love to have it so they 'agree' with the idea and it shoots up the board. But manufacturing knows there are going to be problems producing the new product so they list 'concerns' in the idea details. Product proposes changes to the original idea, which resolves the concern. Without much management oversight, the idea has been identified, prioritized, and fleshed out.
